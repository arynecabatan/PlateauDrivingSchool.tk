Frequently Asked Questions (FAQS)

Why should I choose Plateau Driving School?

Our focus is to develop the students' driving skills in order for them to attain the knowledge, ability and confidence in obtaining a driver's licence. We provide quality service at a very reasonable rate.

How many training sessions are required to become road test ready?

For a new driver, it takes an average of 40 hours. However, it also depends on the student's previous experience and abilities.

Can I use my Driver's Licence issued from another country here in Canada?

Yes, if it is issued by a country which has an exchange agreement with Canada or if you have an International Driving Permit (IDP). Please see this link for more information

How long is a Learner's Permit valid?

A Learner's Permit is valid for 24 months. If expired, it will have to be renewed by taking a knowledge exam.

Can I bring a friend/family during driving lessons?

For insurance and safety reasons, it is discouraged to have a companion during the driving lessons.

How can I book the road test?

You can book appointments for road exams online directly through the ICBC website. (please insert link); or we can book one for you.

Where do we meet for our driving lessons?

We pick you up at a mutually agreed place in the tri-cities, usually your house, train station, school or place of work. Our rates vary if pick-up is beyond the tri-city area.
